How to Play

[1] This is a simple dice game where two players take turns to roll a dice. Each player's goal is to reach a score of 100 or more.

[2] On each turn, a player rolls a dice by clicking the "Roll dice" button. If the player rolls a 1, their current score becomes 0, and it's the next player's turn. If the player rolls any other number, that number is added to their current score.

[3] The player can choose to "Hold" at any time during their turn, which adds their current score to their total score. Then it's the next player's turn.

[4] The game continues until one player reaches a score of 100 or more. That player wins the game!
